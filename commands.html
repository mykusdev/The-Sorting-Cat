Commands

House Commands

The following are house related commands that can be used by everyone.

  • !assignhouse - sorts you into a house
  • !board - shows you the current house leaderboard
  • !krillpoints - shows the points of House Krill
  • !mantapoints - shows the points of House Manta
  • !crabpoints - shows the points of House Crab
  • !jellypoints - shows the points of House Jellyfish



The next few commands can only be used by members who have been assigned the role of "Gives Points".

  • !setall points - sets points of all houses to specified points
  • !addkrill points - adds specified points to House Krill
  • !addmanta points - adds specified points to House Manta
  • !addcrab points - adds specified points to House Crab
  • !addjelly points - adds specified points to House Jellyfish


Miscellaneous Commands

The following commands are just for messing around with your friends on the server.

  • !hug user - hug your friends
  • !yeet user - yeets your friend out of existence
  • !cook user - cooks your friend
  • !eat user - sends a gif of a sky kid eating a chibi

Other than that The Sorting Cat also responds to texts like "Hi Mr Cat", "Good Morning/Evening Mr Cat", and also replies when you meow or bark at it.
